Strategy Guide

Tic-Tac-Toe is a classic two-player game played on a 3x3 grid. The goal is to get three of your symbols in a row, column, or diagonal.

  • The game starts with Player X.
  • Players take turns placing their symbol in an empty cell.
  • First to 3-in-a-row wins.
  • If the grid fills with no winner, it's a draw.
