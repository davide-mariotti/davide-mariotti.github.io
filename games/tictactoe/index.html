About Tic-Tac-Toe

Tic-Tac-Toe is a classic two-player game played on a 3x3 grid. The goal is to get three of your symbols (X or O) in a row, column, or diagonal.

How to Play:

  1. The game starts with Player X.
  2. Players take turns placing their symbol (X or O) in an empty cell on the grid.
  3. The first player to get three of their symbols in a row, column, or diagonal wins.
  4. If all cells are filled and no player has won, the game is a draw.

Click the "Play Again" button to start a new game at any time.
