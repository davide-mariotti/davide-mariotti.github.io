Know Who I'M

Hello there! My name is Davide, and I hail from the beautiful hills of San Miniato, Italy. My journey into the digital world wasn't a straight line; it was fueled by a persistent desire to build and create.

Currently, I am a dedicated Front-End Developer at Adiacent, where I help shape the digital presence of diverse brands through innovative web solutions. At Adiacent, I've transitioned from a builder of sites to an architect of experiences, collaborating with cross-functional teams to deliver projects that range from e-commerce platforms to bespoke internal tools.

Although I haven't graduated from a university, my passion for learning and honing my skills led me to complete specialized courses at esteemed institutions like IED and SSTI in Florence. These provided a solid foundation in design thinking and technical execution, allowing me to bridge the gap between creative vision and code.

As I continue to grow and evolve, I am grateful for the opportunities to apply the knowledge acquired through my educational journey. I am constantly exploring new technologies (React, Astro, Node.js) to stay at the forefront of modern web development.
